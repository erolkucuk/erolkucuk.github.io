Privacy Policy
---------------
Last updated    : January 13,2023
Contact         : erolkucuk@gmail.com


 The application does not collect,store or share any personal data from its users.
 The application gets audio data from the device microphone. Because of this it requires android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO permission to properly run.
This permission allows application record audio data. Audio data got from the microphone is temporarily kept in the device memory and cleared after application closed and is never stored or shared.
