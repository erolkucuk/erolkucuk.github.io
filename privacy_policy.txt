Privacy Policy
---------------
Last updated     : January 16,2023
Application Name : SoundTester
Contact          : erolkucuk@gmail.com


 The application does not collect,store or share any personal data from its users.
 The application records audio data from the device microphone so it requires android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO permission to properly run. This permission allows application record audio data.
 Audio data recorded from the microphone is temporarily kept in the device memory and cleared after application closed and is never stored or shared.
